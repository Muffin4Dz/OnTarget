REVIEW - BMW i8

INTRODUCTION

The BMW i8 revolutionised Sport Cars to a whole NEW level. The i8 is the first sports car with the consumption and emission values of a compact car, while being a Hybrid. With its innovative construction, ground-breaking use of materials, aerodynamic design and wealth of intelligent BMW Efficient Dynamic features, the BMW i8 is just the beginning of these Sport Cars...

PERFORMANCE

The i8 is absolutely on the pace in terms of raw speed, way ahead of it on quality of delivery and unequalled on fuel economy. The power this car provides in the real world is nothing short of excellent. Incredible, especially when you think it’s all coming from a three-cylinder petrol engine and some mains electricity.
